# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Business owners and individual clients looking for high-quality digital solutions (mobile apps, websites, UI/UX designs). They are evaluating technical competence, design sensibility, and reliability.

## Product Purpose
Technova is a software house. The primary purpose of this site is to serve as a digital storefront that attracts clients, builds trust, showcases past work, and drives inquiries. It must demonstrate technical excellence and design capability firsthand.

## Brand Personality
Innovative, trustworthy, professional. Quietly confident.

## Anti-references
- The "SaaS hero-metric" template (big number, small label, gradient accent).
- Saturated AI default scaffolds: tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section (`ABOUT`, `SERVICES`), numbered section markers without real sequence (`01 · About`, `02 · Process`).
- Beige/cream/sand backgrounds masquerading as warmth.
- Generic identical card grids.
- Cluttered, overwhelming, or overly playful designs that undermine the "trustworthy" and "professional" traits.

## Design Principles
- **Practice what you preach**: As a software house, the site itself must be a flawless demonstration of the capabilities being sold—fast, accessible, and beautifully crafted.
- **Expert confidence**: Communicate through restrained typography, deliberate pacing, and high-quality interactions rather than loud marketing gimmicks.
- **Show, don't tell**: Let the work (portfolio, case studies, micro-interactions) speak for the quality of the service.
- **Intentional motion**: Use motion to guide the eye and provide feedback, never just because it's possible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility (WCAG AA). Ensure sufficient contrast (especially for muted text), readable line lengths, and support for reduced motion preferences.
