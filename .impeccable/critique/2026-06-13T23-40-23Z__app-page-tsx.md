#### Overall Impression
A highly polished, premium landing page that confidently executes the "twilight lab" aesthetic. It's ready to ship visually, though it could benefit from some intentional motion design to make it feel alive.

#### What's Working
- **Confident Typography**: The H2s anchor the sections perfectly without needing tiny uppercase labels to explain what they are.
- **Accessibility**: The form inputs now respect keyboard users with proper `focus-visible` rings while remaining sleek for mouse users.
- **Restraint**: The OKLCH color palette (obsidian/cyan) is used effectively, with the accent color driving interaction rather than just decorating.

#### Priority Issues
- **[P3] Lack of Entrance Motion**
  - **Why it matters**: A site positioning itself as a high-end engineering studio should feel dynamic. Right now, it's a bit static.
  - **Fix**: Introduce subtle, staggered reveal animations on scroll.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable animate`

- **[P3] Form Interaction Design**
  - **Why it matters**: The form looks great but lacks interactive feedback (validation, loading states, success messages).
  - **Fix**: Hook up the form to a simple state machine and add error/success UI.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable harden`
