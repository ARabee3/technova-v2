### Overall Impression
A highly polished, premium interface that successfully captures a dark, laboratory aesthetic. The floating pill navbar is a massive improvement. The only remaining sore thumb is the gradient text in the contact section.

#### What's Working
1. **Floating Pill Navbar**: The `max-w-5xl` pill sitting over the hero noise texture is visually striking and solves the logo aspect ratio elegantly.
2. **Contact Form Hardening**: The form handles submission states, validation, and layout cleanly.

#### Priority Issues

- **[P1] Gradient Text Anti-Pattern**
  - **Location**: `app/components/ContactForm.tsx` (Line 68)
  - **Why it matters**: Gradient text (`bg-clip-text`) reduces legibility and instantly tags the design as "AI-generated" or cheap SaaS.
  - **Fix**: Remove the gradient and replace it with a solid brand color (e.g., `text-ink` or `text-white`). Let the weight and size do the typographic work.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable quieter` or `/impeccable typeset`
