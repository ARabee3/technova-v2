#### Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment**: The aesthetic foundation is very strong—the OKLCH color palette, stark typography, and blueprint motifs establish a clear, premium identity. However, the design falls into the "AI scaffolding" trap: **every single section features a tiny, uppercase, tracked eyebrow** ("Technova Digital Studio", "What We Build", "How We Deliver", "Proven Results", "Our Philosophy", "Ready to scale?"). This repetitive cadence is the clearest tell of an AI-generated page. Additionally, both the Services and Process sections rely on numbered list patterns (`01, 02, 03`), compounding the template feel.

**Deterministic scan**: The automated detector returned `0` findings. The technical markup hygiene is excellent (no disallowed gradient text, no side-stripe borders).

#### Overall Impression
A visually striking, highly professional landing page that is let down by structural repetition. The blueprint/terminal aesthetic is executed well, but the page needs its "AI scaffolding" stripped away to feel truly bespoke.

#### What's Working
- **Cohesive Aesthetic**: The matte obsidian background, hairline grid borders, and sharp cyan accents create a highly credible "engineering studio" feel.
- **Hero Simplification**: The newly implemented "plain English" terminal log strikes a great balance between technical flavor and approachable business value.
- **Portfolio Section**: Using real, high-quality images with `mix-blend-luminosity` and a cyan hover reveal is a premium, deliberate design move.

#### Priority Issues
- **[P1] Repetitive Section Eyebrows**
  - **Why it matters**: Repeating a tiny tracked uppercase label above every single `h2` makes the page read like a generic template rather than a deliberate narrative. It's the #1 AI slop tell.
  - **Fix**: Remove the eyebrows from `Services`, `Portfolio`, `About`, and `Footer`. Let the typography of the primary headings carry the weight. Keep it only if it serves as a strict structural marker (e.g., just in the Hero).
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable typeset` or `/impeccable bolder`

- **[P2] Invalid Tailwind Class in Form**
  - **Why it matters**: Line 545 uses `className="font-maxLength text-[10px]..."` for a form label, which is an invalid Tailwind utility, breaking the intended monospace styling.
  - **Fix**: Replace `font-maxLength` with `font-mono`.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable polish`

- **[P2] Redundant Numbering Scaffolding**
  - **Why it matters**: Numbering the `Process` steps (`01, 02, 03, 04`) makes sense. Numbering the `Services` cards (`01, 02, 03`) right above it feels like lazy scaffolding, especially when they don't represent a sequence.
  - **Fix**: Remove the numbers from the Services cards and rely on the grid layout and typography alone.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable distill`

- **[P2] Accessibility of Form Focus States**
  - **Why it matters**: The form inputs use `focus:outline-none focus:ring-0`, completely overriding the browser's default focus ring in favor of a subtle `border-b` color change. This fails accessibility guidelines for keyboard users.
  - **Fix**: Retain or implement a proper `focus-visible` ring or stronger visual indicator.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable audit`

#### Persona Red Flags

**Sam (Accessibility)**: 
- Form inputs remove the default focus ring (`focus:outline-none`) and only change the bottom border color on focus, which may not be visible enough for low-vision keyboard users.

**Casey (Mobile User)**:
